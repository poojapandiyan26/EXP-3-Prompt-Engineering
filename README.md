# Introduction

Prompt Engineering is the process of designing and optimizing instructions given to Artificial Intelligence (AI) systems to obtain accurate, relevant, and meaningful outputs. It involves carefully selecting words, providing context, defining constraints, and structuring prompts in a way that helps AI models understand user requirements effectively.

With the rapid advancement of Generative AI technologies, Prompt Engineering has become one of the most important skills in modern AI applications. Properly designed prompts improve communication between humans and machines, enhance productivity, reduce ambiguity, and increase the quality of AI-generated responses.

Different AI platforms respond differently to prompts based on their architecture, training methods, and contextual understanding. Therefore, this experiment compares multiple AI systems to analyze their effectiveness in handling the same prompt.
